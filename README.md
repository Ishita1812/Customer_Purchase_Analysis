# Customer Purchase Analysis Dashboard --

1. **Project Title / Headline** :
Analyzing 3,900+ retail transactions to uncover customer behavior, revenue drivers and business growth opportunities.

2. **Description** :
An end-to-end data analytics project that transforms raw customer transaction data into actionable business insights. The project leverages Pandas for data cleaning and feature engineering, MySQL for business-focused analysis and Power BI for interactive dashboarding. Key analyses include customer segmentation, revenue trends, subscription behavior, discount effectiveness and product performance, enabling data-driven decision-making.

3. **Tech Stack** :
🐍 Python (Pandas, NumPy)
🗄️ MySQL
📊 Power BI
📈 Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering
📋 Business Analytics & Dashboarding

4. **Data Source** : 
Customer Shopping Trends Dataset from the public dataset repository on Kaggle.
This dataset containing transaction, demographic and purchase history data which was used to simulate real-world retail analytics and business intelligence workflows.

5. **Features & Highlights** :
Cleaned and transformed 3,900+ customer transactions using Pandas.
Built a MySQL analytics database for business-driven SQL insights.
Performed customer segmentation into New, Returning and Loyal shoppers.
Analyzed revenue drivers, subscription trends and discount effectiveness.
Identified top-performing products and high-value customer segments.
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard with dynamic KPIs and filters.
Delivered actionable recommendations for customer retention and revenue growth.

6. **Business Problem** :
Retail businesses generate large volumes of customer transaction data but often struggle to identify purchasing patterns, customer segments, and revenue drivers. This project aims to transform raw shopping data into actionable insights that support customer retention, targeted marketing, and revenue growth.

7. **Key Business Questions** :
Which customer segments generate the most revenue?
How does subscription status impact customer spending?
Which products and categories drive the highest sales?
How effective are discounts in influencing purchases?
Which age groups contribute most to overall revenue?
Are repeat customers more likely to become subscribers?

8. **Key Insights** :
Clothing generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
Young Adult customers contributed the largest share of sales and revenue.
Non-subscribers outspent subscribers, indicating that subscription benefits may not be sufficiently driving customer value.
Several products showed a high dependency on discounts to generate purchases.
Repeat customers demonstrated stronger purchasing behavior and loyalty.
Product ratings highlighted top-performing items for promotional campaigns.

9. **Business Impact** :
Identified high-value customer segments for targeted marketing.
Revealed opportunities to redesign the subscription program and improve its perceived value.
Supported optimization of discount strategies to balance sales and profitability.
Highlighted top-performing product categories for inventory and marketing decisions.
